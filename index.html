The animation industry went through huge transformations in the late 1920s and the early 1930s with the invention of three-strip Technicolor and synchronized sounds. Many iconic characters and record-breaking films were born during these years.
Let's look into what happened during the years Mickey Mouse, Betty Boop, Popeye, and many of our favorite cartoon stars were created!
